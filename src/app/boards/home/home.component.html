United Nations 15th goal "Life on Lands" deals with protecting, restoring and promoting sustainable use of ecosystems and halt bio diversity loss. Plastic waste entering fresh water sources such as rivers and ultimately ending in oceans poses a threat to ecosystems. This plastic waste can cause problems for wild life and fish, which can have adverse effects on biodiversity in the ecosystem.

Hence, Our solutions deal with removing the plastic andother forms of litter from water ways to save the biodiversity in that ecosystem. We thought “If we can have rubbish bins on land, then why not have them in the ocean?”. So “AquaSieve“ is a rubbish bin like floating device which filters water from a river or sea and collects plastic litter. We think this device will make a huge impact on the fight to save biodiversity.
